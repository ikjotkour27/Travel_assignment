Welcome to the Valley

The Crown Jewel of the Himalayas

Often called "Paradise on Earth", Srinagar is the summer capital of Jammu & Kashmir. Famous for its shimmering lakes, wooden houseboats, saffron fields and centuries-old gardens, the city blends gentle beauty with thrilling mountain adventure.

On the water

Shikara & Houseboats

Float past floating gardens and the famous floating market, then spend the night in a hand-carved cedar houseboat.

Adventure

Gulmarg Snow & Skiing

Ride one of the world's highest cable cars and carve down powder slopes in one of Asia's finest ski resorts.

Heritage

Mughal Gardens

Stroll the terraced lawns of Nishat and Shalimar Bagh, designed by emperors and framed by the Zabarwan range.

Valleys Beyond the City

Srinagar is the perfect base for day trips into the wider Kashmir Valley. Picnic beside the Lidder River in Pahalgam, trek the golden meadows of Sonmarg, or chase the spring tulips with the lake glittering behind you.

Every season paints the valley a different colour — pink blossoms in spring, emerald meadows in summer and fiery chinar leaves in autumn.
