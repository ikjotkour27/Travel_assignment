Plan Your Trip

Practical tips for a smooth Kashmir adventure

Before you go

When to Visit

Srinagar is beautiful all year round — your ideal season depends on what you want to see and do.

Mar – May

Spring

Mild days, blossoming orchards and the famous tulip garden in full bloom. The greenest, most colourful time to visit.

Jun – Aug

Summer

Warm, pleasant weather perfect for shikara rides, garden walks and trekking in Sonmarg and Pahalgam.

Sep – Nov

Autumn

The chinar trees turn gold and crimson. Crisp, quiet and wonderfully photogenic — a favourite of returning travellers.

Dec – Feb

Winter

Snow blankets the valley and Gulmarg becomes a world-class ski resort. Cold, magical and ideal for snow sports.

Getting around

How to Reach Srinagar

✈ By Air

Srinagar International Airport (SXR) connects to Delhi, Mumbai and other major Indian cities with daily flights.

🚌 By Road

Scenic national highways link Srinagar to Jammu and beyond. Shared taxis and buses run regularly through the mountains.

🚹 Local Travel

Use auto-rickshaws and taxis within the city, and a shikara to explore the lake. Day-trip cabs are easy to arrange.

Good to know

Travel FAQ

What should I pack?

Layers are key — even summer evenings are cool. Bring warm clothing in winter, comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen and a light raincoat in spring.

How many days do I need?

Four to six days lets you enjoy the lake, the gardens and at least one mountain day trip without rushing.

Is it suitable for families?

Absolutely. Shikara rides, gardens and the gondola are gentle and enjoyable for all ages.
